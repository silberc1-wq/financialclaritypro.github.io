FC PRO Communications Platform FC PRO Communications Platform FC PRO LLC provides communication and notification systems for client organizations. The platform is used to send operational notifications, appointment reminders, report confirmations, order confirmations, community alerts, and informational updates. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Service 1: RSK Report System Authorized financial coaches and staff members submit reports through the platform and receive confirmation messages acknowledging receipt of their reports. Sample Messages: Report received successfully. Reference #12345. Your report has been submitted and recorded. Thank you. Your report has been received. Opt-In: Authorized coaches provide their phone numbers directly to the organization and are approved by administrators before using the system. Service 2: Order Notification System Customers may submit orders through SMS and receive confirmations regarding receipt, processing, and status updates. Sample Messages: Order received. Order #10045. Your order has been successfully received. Your order is being processed. Opt-In: Customers voluntarily provide their phone numbers while placing orders or requesting services. Service 3: Community & Shul Messaging Organizations, synagogues, and community groups use the platform to send informational notifications to members. Sample Messages: Mincha today is at 6:45 PM. Shiur tonight begins at 8:00 PM. Community event reminder for this evening. Important community notification. Opt-In: Members voluntarily subscribe through organization registration forms, membership forms, community sign-up forms, or by texting JOIN when available. Opt-In Methods JOIN START SUBSCRIBE Opt-Out Users may opt out at any time by replying: STOP STOPALL UNSUBSCRIBE CANCEL END QUIT Users who opt out will no longer receive messages from the service unless they re-subscribe.
