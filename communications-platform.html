Automation & Communication Platform

FC PRO LLC provides automation, reporting, and communication systems for businesses, schools, community organizations, and client organizations.

The FC PRO LLC communication platform helps organizations send informational notifications to people who have agreed to receive messages. These systems are designed for practical operational communication, not third-party marketing.

Who Uses These Systems

Businesses, schools, community groups, service organizations, and internal teams can use the platform to communicate with members, customers, staff, or clients who provided consent to receive messages.

Consent and Opt-In

Users opt in by providing their phone number through organization membership forms, registration forms, appointment forms, order forms, signup forms, or other written consent forms provided by the organization.

Users may also opt in by texting JOIN to the organization's designated phone number. A confirmation message is sent before informational SMS notifications begin. After confirmation, the user is added to the approved messaging list.

By providing a phone number and opting in, users agree to receive informational messages related to the organization, including notifications, reminders, updates, confirmations, and service-related communications.

Opt-Out and Help

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Users may request help by replying HELP or COMMANDS.
