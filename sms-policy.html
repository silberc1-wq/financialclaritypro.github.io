SMS Policy

This page explains how users opt in to receive SMS messages from FC PRO LLC communication systems, how to get help, and how to opt out.

FC PRO LLC provides automation, reporting, and communication systems for businesses, schools, community organizations, and client organizations. SMS messages sent through these systems are informational and operational. They are not used for third-party marketing.

SMS Program Notice
Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP or COMMANDS for assistance.

Types of Messages

  • Community and organization announcements
  • Event reminders and schedule updates
  • Emergency notifications
  • Appointment reminders
  • Order confirmations and order updates
  • Report confirmations and service-related notifications

How Users Opt In

Users may opt in by completing a membership, registration, signup, appointment, order, or other written consent form provided by the organization. The form includes a phone number field and SMS consent language. By providing a mobile phone number and submitting the form, the user consents to receive informational SMS messages related to the organization or service.

Users may also opt in by texting JOIN to the organization's designated phone number. A confirmation message is sent before informational SMS notifications begin. After confirmation, the user is added to the approved messaging list.

Opt-In Confirmation Message

Welcome to FC PRO LLC notifications. You have successfully subscribed to receive informational SMS messages. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

Opt-Out Instructions

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Other supported opt-out keywords may include OPTOUT, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, REVOKE, and STOPALL.

You have successfully been unsubscribed. You will not receive any more messages from this number. Reply START to resubscribe.

Help Instructions

Users may request help by replying HELP, INFO, or COMMANDS.

FC PRO LLC Alerts: For assistance contact support@financialclaritypro.com. Message frequency varies. Msg & Data Rates May Apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Privacy

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.

Terms

Use of the SMS program is subject to our Terms & Conditions.

Contact
