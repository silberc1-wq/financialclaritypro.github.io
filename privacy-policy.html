Privacy Policy

FC PRO LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information related to our communication and messaging services.

Information We Collect

We may collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, organization information, message preferences, and communication history when users interact with FC PRO LLC or one of our client organizations.

How We Use Information

We use this information to send informational messages, community notifications, appointment reminders, order updates, service updates, and other organization-related communications.

SMS Messaging

By providing a phone number through a registration form, membership form, order form, appointment form, signup form, or by texting an opt-in keyword when available, users consent to receive SMS messages from FC PRO LLC or its client organizations.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

No Sale of Personal Information

FC PRO LLC does not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or SMS consent information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Sharing

Information may be shared with service providers only as needed to operate communication systems, send messages, process requests, or maintain our services.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information.

Opt-Out

Users may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. After opting out, users will no longer receive SMS messages from that number unless they opt in again.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact FC PRO LLC through our website at financialclaritypro.com.
